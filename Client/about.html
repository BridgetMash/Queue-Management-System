About Us - Virtual Queue Management System

Our Mission

  • Patient Comfort: To minimise the discomfort of waiting and maximize patient satisfaction.
  • Efficient Care: To prioritise critical cases while ensuring that each patient is attended to as quickly as possible.
  • Transparency: To provide real-time updates and complete transparency in the queue progress, ensuring that patients and their families are always informed.

How It Works

  • Check-In: Upon arrival, patients can easily check-in through our kiosks or mobile application.
  • Virtual Queuing: Patients are assigned a virtual queue number, allowing them to wait in the comfort of our lounge or even outside the premises.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Receive regular updates on your queue status via SMS or our mobile app.
  • Personalised Care: Our system integrates with medical records to provide doctors with immediate access to essential information, ensuring personalised and efficient care.

Why Choose Our VQMS?

  • Convenience: No need to stand in line; wait comfortably until it's your turn.
  • Accessibility: Check your queue status at any time from any location.
  • Environmentally Friendly: A paperless system reducing our carbon footprint.
